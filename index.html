Services

These days, every business needs a web presence, but most don't have the time, resources, or know-how to make a site on their own.

I create fast, optimized, custom websites that fit your business needs. Get exactly what you need, whether that's a simple one page site or a complex web application.

Price is set per project, so you'll know the full cost before agreeing to anything - no hidden fees!

When you're ready to get started, the first step is to contact me to discuss your project.

How It Works

  1. Initial call

    We'll meet and discuss what you're looking for, including, the scope of your project, your timeline, and your budget.

    Typically this takes place over Zoom, but if you're in the Ottawa area I can come to you.

    Contact me to schedule this call.

  2. Proposal

    I'll create a project outline, including a mockup of the proposed site, a clear description of the features, and the price and schedule for the full project.

    You decide if this will work for you - no pressure to commit if it isn't what you're looking for!

  3. Project Contract

    If you're happy with the proposal, we'll both sign a project contract.

    The project contract puts everything in writing: the details of the site, the timeline, the price, etc. It explains who is responsible for what, and what will happen if anything changes.

    [I do need a deposit before starting work on the site.]

  4. Site Creation

    I'll give you regular updates as I create the site. I want to create something you'll love, so you'll have lots of opportunities for feedback.

  5. Site Launch

    When everything is complete and you're happy with the site, I'll launch it!

    From there, the site is all yours! I'll transfer the site code and the hosting to you.

  6. Ongoing Support (Optional)

    If you choose, I offer an option for ongoing support: a regular fee guarantees some of my time set aside for you each month. This is totally optional, and you can opt out at any point.

    I can update anything on your site, fix any technical issues you run into, redesign small details, etc.

About

I'm Kerry Smart, a full stack developer based near Ottawa, Ontario.

I love to work on projects that solve real problems - even if the problem is as simple as "I need a website!"

I'm particularly interested in projects that combine technology and art or textiles. I'm currently available for part-time and contract work, but will be open to full time work around early 2023.

My education is in Knowledge Integration, Math, and Fine Art. Prior to freelancing, I primarily worked in education technology but have also had a smattering of other jobs, including teaching knitting.

In my spare time, I love making textile art, walking in Gatineau Park, and collecting art and ceramics.
