Why pay for a site when I can use a website builder?

This is the most common question I get! Website builders can work for some people, but there are some real disadvantages.

The biggest disadvantage is price: most website builders have costly monthly fees that can add up quickly. I price per project, so you'll always know how much you're paying up front.

Most website builders have limited options and have you select a premade style, with limited ways to change it. The websites I make are completely customized to you, so you get exactly what you want - no detail is too small to change.

There's often a steep learning curve: it can take so long to figure out the software that it costs you more in time than you would have paid for a custom site. Adding extra features like scheduling or online store can get even more complicated, and mistakes can have a real financial impact on your business. And of course, something always seems to go wrong when you need to make an update in a hurry! I offer a retainer option that ensures I'll always have time to make a quick update for you.

How it works

  1. Initial call

    We'll discuss your needs, the scope of your project, your timeline, and your budget. Typically this takes place over Zoom, but if you're in the Ottawa area I can come to you. Contact me to schedule this call.

  2. Proposal

    I'll create a project outline, including a mockup of the proposed site, a clear description of the features, and the price and fee schedule for the full project. You decide if this will work for you - no pressure to commit if it isn't what you're looking for!

  3. Deposit

    We'll both sign a project contract, and you'll send me an initial desposit. Once I recieve your payment, I'll get to work on your site.

  4. Site Creation

    I'll give you regular updates as I create the site. I want to create something you'll love, so you'll have lots of opportunities for feedback.

  5. Site Launch

    When everything is complete, it's all yours: I'll transfer the site and the hosting to you.

  6. Ongoing Support (Optional)

    If you choose, I offer an option for ongoing support: a regular fee guarantees some of my time set aside for you each month. I can update anything on your site, or fix any technical issues you run into.
