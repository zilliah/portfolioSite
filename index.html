Why should I hire you when I can use a site builder?

Site builders can be useful tools, but they aren't for everyone. If a site builder works for you, that's great, but they do come with a lot of disadvantages:

  • Price: most site builders charge a monthly fee, which adds up to a lot of money over time. When I make you a site, you pay once and it's yours forever. One-time fees have much less of an impact on your business.
  • Customization: a lot of website builders offer limited customization options, and you may be stuck using templates that don't work for you. With a custom website you can have it exactly how you want it.
  • Ease of use: even the easiest website builder comes with a learning curve, and more advanced builders can be almost as hard as writing code yourself.
  • Time: when you have a small business, it can feel like there just aren't enough hours in the day to get everything done. Creating and maintaining a website takes time
  • Predictability: things can change quickly on the internet, and there's no guarantee that your site builder will be around forever. If they go out of business abruptly or get bought out, you could lose your site entirely

  3. Planning

    We'll discuss what you're looking for, including your budget, timeline, and what you want on your site. We can meet in person if you live near Ottawa, over Zoom, or even do everything by email.
