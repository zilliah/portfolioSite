How It Works

  1. Initial call

    We'll meet and discuss what you're looking for, including, the scope of your project, your timeline, and your budget.

    Typically this takes place over Zoom, but if you're in the Ottawa area I can come to you.

    Contact me to schedule this call.

  2. Proposal

    I'll create a project outline, including a mockup of the proposed site, a clear description of the features, and the price and schedule for the full project.

  3. Planning

    We'll discuss what you're looking for, including your budget, timeline, and what you want on your site. We can meet in person if you live near Ottawa, over Zoom, or even do everything by email.

    I'll create a project proposal with a basic mockup of the site, clear descriptions of everything to include, and price and deadline for the finished site.

    You decide if this will work for you - no pressure to commit if it isn't what you're looking for!

    If you're happy with the proposal, we'll both sign a project contract. The project contract puts everything in writing: the details of the site, the timeline, the price, etc. It explains who is responsible for what, and what will happen if anything changes.

  4. Site Creation

    I'll give you regular updates as I create the site. I want to create something you'll love, so you'll have lots of opportunities for feedback.

  5. Site Launch

    When everything is complete and you're happy with the site, I'll launch it!

    From there, the site is all yours! I'll transfer the site code and the hosting to you.

  6. Ongoing Support (Optional)

    If you choose, I offer an option for ongoing support: a regular fee guarantees some of my time set aside for you each month. This is totally optional, and you can opt out at any point.

    I can update anything on your site, fix any technical issues you run into, redesign small details, etc.
