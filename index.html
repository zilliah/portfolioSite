How it works

  1. Initial call

    We'll discuss your needs, the scope of your project, your timeline, and your budget.

  2. Proposal

    I'll create a project outline, including a mockup of the proposed site, and the price for the full project. You decide if this will work for you - no pressure to commit if it isn't what you're looking for!

  3. Deposit

    We'll both sign a project contract, and you'll send me an initial despoit. Once I recieve your payment, I'll start working on your site.

  4. Site Creation

    I'll give you regular updates as I create the site. I want to create something you'll love, so you'll have lots of opportunities for feedback.

  5. Site Launch

    When everything is complete, it's all yours: I'll transfer the site and the hosting to you.

  6. Ongoing Support (Optional)

    If you choose, I offer an option for ongoing support: a regular fee guarantees some of my time set aside for you each month. I can update anything on your site, or deal with any tech issues that pop up.
